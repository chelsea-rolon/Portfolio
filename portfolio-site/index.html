About Me

Get to Know Me

Hi! I’m Chelsea. I am a Python developer and UX-certified full stack software developer who specializes in bridging the gap between data-driven logic, human-centered design, and impactful software. Before transitioning into software development, I spent 8 years working remotely and independently as a Physical Therapist Assistant. During this time, I spearheaded a comprehensive fall prevention program where I conducted user interviews and surveys, analyzed data to uncover critical patterns in patient falls, and delivered data-driven presentations to ALF staff and patients. This experience ignited my passion for UX design and data analysis, leading me to earn my UX Certification and dive deep into Python programming. My diverse background makes me a unique asset to any development team. I don’t just write clean, logical backend code; I approach every project with an 8-year foundation in empathetic patient care, a structured UX research framework, and a proven track record of managing independent, remote projects from concept to execution. Whether I am optimizing data pipelines with Pandas, designing intuitive user flows, or designing for large-scale creative events, I thrive on turning complex problems into elegant, user-friendly solutions.
